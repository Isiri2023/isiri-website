About Us

Founded in Maharashtra, India, ISIRI is a trusted name in the seed industry. We began as a domestic supplier, serving farmers across India with reliable and high-yielding seeds. Thanks to the dedication of our team and the trust of our partners, we are now expanding our reach to the international market.

Our mission is simple: to empower farmers around the globe with premium-quality seeds that produce healthy crops and sustainable yields. We work closely with agronomists and researchers to select seed varieties suited to different climates and soil conditions.

While our website is still under construction, you can expect to find detailed information about our processes, certifications, and team soon. We invite you to check back regularly or reach out directly via our contact page.
